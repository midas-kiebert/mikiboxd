MiKiNO Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This page explains how users of MiKiNO can delete their account and associated data. MiKiNO is operated by Midas Kiebert.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open MiKiNO and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger zone.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate in active systems once you confirm.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Account profile data (email, display name, preferences).
  • Friend relationships, friend requests, and friend groups.
  • Showtime selections, invites/pings, visibility settings, and saved presets.
  • Stored push notification tokens linked to your account.

What May Be Retained Temporarily

  • Application logs may be retained for up to 30 days.
  • If backup systems are enabled by infrastructure providers, backup data may remain until backup rotation expires.
  • Data may be retained longer where required by applicable law.

Delete Data Without Deleting Account

MiKiNO currently does not offer partial data deletion independent of account deletion. To delete associated personal data, you must delete your account.
