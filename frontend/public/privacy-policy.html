MiKiNO Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MiKiNO ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares personal data when you use the MiKiNO mobile app and web app (collectively, the "Service").

MiKiNO is operated by Midas Kiebert, who is the data controller for personal data processed through the Service.

1. Data We Collect

Depending on how you use MiKiNO, we may process:

  • Account data: email address, display name/username, and encrypted password data.
  • Profile settings: notification preferences, notification delivery channel preferences, optional Letterboxd username, and visibility preferences (such as incognito mode).
  • Social and activity data: friends, friend requests, showtime status selections (for example going/interested), optional seat details, showtime invites (pings), visibility selections, friend groups, and saved presets/filters.
  • Watchlist sync data: if you connect a Letterboxd username, we fetch and store watchlist-related references used to filter your experience.
  • Notification data: push notification token and platform (iOS/Android/Web) used to deliver notifications you enable.
  • Diagnostics: error and performance telemetry when error monitoring is enabled in our backend environment.
  • Local app storage: authentication token stored on your device (secure storage on mobile, local storage on web).

2. How We Use Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide MiKiNO core features (showtimes, social features, presets, watchlist filtering).
  • To deliver the notifications you choose (push and/or email).
  • To secure the Service, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To operate and improve product reliability and performance.

Providing core account data (such as email address and password data) is necessary to create and use an account. If you do not provide this data, you cannot use account-based features of the Service.

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, we rely on these legal bases:

  • Contract: to create your account and provide requested app functionality.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure the Service, prevent abuse, debug issues, and improve reliability.
  • Consent: for optional device notification permissions and similar optional features.
  • Legal obligations: where required by law.

4. Data Sharing and Processors

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers that process data on our behalf, such as:

  • Push delivery provider: Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (Android push notifications you enable).
  • Email delivery provider (for account and notification emails if configured).
  • Error monitoring provider (for crash/error diagnostics, if enabled).
  • Infrastructure and hosting providers (to run the Service).

If you add a Letterboxd username, MiKiNO accesses publicly available watchlist information linked to that username for watchlist-related features.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and feature data is retained while your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, we delete your account record and associated data from active systems immediately, subject to legal obligations.
  • Application logs are retained for up to 30 days.
  • If crash/error monitoring is enabled, diagnostic events are retained according to the provider's retention configuration.
  • If infrastructure backups are enabled, backup retention is determined by provider backup settings and operational/security needs.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update profile information and preferences in app settings.
  • You can disable notifications in app settings and your device settings.
  • You can delete your account in app settings.

If GDPR applies, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability. Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting processing based on consent before withdrawal. You can exercise your rights by contacting us at privacy@mikino.nl.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. If you are in the Netherlands, this is the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens): autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

MiKiNO is primarily intended for adults. In the EEA (including the Netherlands), users should be at least 16 years old, or the minimum age required by local law to provide valid consent for personal data processing. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law.

9. International Transfers

Some service providers may process personal data outside the EEA. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and/or transfers to countries covered by an adequacy decision.

For Android push notifications, MiKiNO uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google). Depending on Google's processing setup, related data may be processed outside the EEA with appropriate safeguards in place.

You may request more information about transfer safeguards by contacting privacy@mikino.nl.

10. Automated Decision-Making

MiKiNO does not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects or similarly significant effects for users.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date.
