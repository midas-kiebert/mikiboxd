MiKiNO Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MiKiNO ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares personal data when you use the MiKiNO mobile app and web app (collectively, the "Service").

MiKiNO is operated by Midas Kiebert, who is the data controller for personal data processed through the Service.

1. Data We Collect

Depending on how you use MiKiNO, we may process:

  • Account data: email address, display name/username, and encrypted password data.
  • Profile settings: notification preferences, notification delivery channel preferences, optional Letterboxd username, and visibility preferences (such as incognito mode).
  • Social and activity data: friends, friend requests, showtime status selections (for example going/interested), optional seat details, showtime invites (pings), visibility selections, friend groups, and saved presets/filters.
  • Watchlist sync data: if you connect a Letterboxd username, we fetch and store watchlist-related references used to filter your experience.
  • Notification data: push notification token and platform (iOS/Android/Web) used to deliver notifications you enable.
  • Diagnostics: error and performance telemetry when error monitoring is enabled in our backend environment.
  • Local app storage: authentication token stored on your device (secure storage on mobile, local storage on web).

2. How We Use Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide MiKiNO core features (showtimes, social features, presets, watchlist filtering).
  • To deliver the notifications you choose (push and/or email).
  • To secure the Service, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To operate and improve product reliability and performance.

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, we rely on:

  • Contract: to provide the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure and improve the Service.
  • Consent: for device notification permissions and similar optional features.
  • Legal obligations: where required by law.

4. Data Sharing and Processors

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers that process data on our behalf, such as:

  • Push delivery provider (for push notifications you enable).
  • Email delivery provider (for account and notification emails if configured).
  • Error monitoring provider (for crash/error diagnostics, if enabled).
  • Infrastructure and hosting providers (to run the Service).

If you add a Letterboxd username, MiKiNO accesses publicly available watchlist information linked to that username for watchlist-related features.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and feature data is retained while your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, we delete your account record and associated data from active systems, subject to legal/operational retention needs.
  • Some operational logs and backups may persist for a limited period.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update profile information and preferences in app settings.
  • You can disable notifications in app settings and your device settings.
  • You can delete your account in app settings.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

MiKiNO is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own through our service providers. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date.
